In 1978 and 1979, President Jimmy Carter commuted their sentences to time served, and the four returned to Puerto Rico.The revolt of October 1950 failed because of the overwhelming force used by the U.S. military, the Puerto Rican National Guard (the 296th Regiment, a component of the United States National Guard), the FBI, the CIA, and the Puerto Rican Insular Police, all of whom were aligned against the Nationalists.
