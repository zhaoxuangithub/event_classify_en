USSTRATCOM was established in 1992 as a successor to Strategic Air Command (SAC).It is charged with space operations (such as military satellites), information operations (such as information warfare), missile defense, global command and control, intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (C4ISR), global strike and strategic deterrence (the United States nuclear arsenal), and combating weapons of mass destruction.Flying and notable non-flying units based at Offutt Air Force Base.
