Hussein 's regime had proven to be a problem for the UN and Iraq 's neighbors due to its use of chemical weapons against Iranians and Kurds in the 1980s . Following the ceasefire agreement that suspended hostilities ( but not officially ended ) in the 1991 Gulf War , the United States and its allies instituted and began patrolling Iraqi no-fly zones , to protect Iraq 's Kurdish and Shi'a Arab population — both of which suffered attacks from the Hussein regime before and after the Gulf War — in Iraq 's northern and southern regions , respectively .
