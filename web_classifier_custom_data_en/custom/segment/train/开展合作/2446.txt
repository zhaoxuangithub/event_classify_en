He was also a university professor in Ferrara ( 1964–1966 ) , Padua ( 1966–1970 ) and in other foreign universities . He often collaborated with the UN : he was a member of the commission for International Trade Law ( 1969–1972 ) and of the non-governmental organization of the United Nations ( 1986–1994 ) , of which he is currently honorary president . Elected to the Chamber of Deputiesin 1994 with the Pole of Freedoms , he served Minister of Foreign Trade in the first Berlusconi government .
