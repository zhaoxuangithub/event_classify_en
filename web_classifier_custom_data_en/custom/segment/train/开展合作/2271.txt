[ 29 ] A resolution on cooperation between the United Nations and the IPU allowed for circulation of official IPU documents in the General Assembly . UN and the IPU cooperate closely in various fields , in particular peace and security , economic and social development , international law , human rights , and democracy and gender issues , but IPU has not obtained the status of UN General Assembly subsidiary organ . The fourth world conference on UN 70th anniversary marked by Ban Ki-Moon as " UN70 " was organised in September 2015 where Speakers of all IPU(Inter-Parliamentary Union ) member parliaments and of non-member parliaments were invited from across the world .
