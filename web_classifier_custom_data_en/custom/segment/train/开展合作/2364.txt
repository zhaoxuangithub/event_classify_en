[ Note 1 ] Under this treaty the signatories , referred to as the five powers , agreed to collaborate in the defence ﬁeld as well as in the political , economic and cultural ﬁelds . During the Korean War ( 1950–1953 ) , the headquarters , personnel and plans of the WU 's defence arm , the Western Union Defence Organisation ( WUDO ) , were transferred to the newly established North Atlantic Treaty Organisation ( NATO ) , providing the nucleus of NATO 's command structure at the Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe ( SHAPE ) .
