Economically , the creation of the Prussian Zollverein customs union in 1818 , and its subsequent expansion to include other states of the nominally Austrian-led German Confederation , reduced competition between and within the states . Despite the legal , administrative , and political disruption associated with the end of the Holy Roman Empire , the people of the German-speaking areas of the old Empire had a common linguistic , cultural , and legal tradition further enhanced by their shared experience in the French Revolutionary Wars and Napoleonic Wars .
