Subsequently , the Soviet Union refused to recognize the union , and supported an intense propaganda stating that the Kingdom of Romania was an imperialistic multi-ethnic state . Bessarabia was a part of Romania until 1940 when the USSR re-annexed the territory as well as Northern Bukovina . The convention of October 28 , 1920 , whereby the British Empire , France , Italy , and Japan recognized Romanian sovereignty in Bessarabia , was rejected as invalid by the USSR .
