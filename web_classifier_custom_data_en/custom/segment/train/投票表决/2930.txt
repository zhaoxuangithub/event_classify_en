[3]The passage of the referendum would have removed Tokelau from the United Nations list of Non-Self-Governing Territories , as the Cook Islands and Niue were removed from this list when they were granted self-governance in 1965 and 1974 , respectively . Outgoing Tokelau Ulu ( head of government ) Pio Tuia suggested in February 2006 that since the vote failed to pass by such a small margin , the issue was likely to be revisited in a few years ' time .
