In the 2017 General Election , around 35 members of the Young Greens stood for election to Westminster , including former Co-Chair Hannah Ellen Clare ( Harlow , 660 votes ) , former Executive Committee members Alice Kiff ( Birmingham Edgbaston , 562 votes ) and Arran Rangi ( Ashfield , 398 votes ) , and current Executive Committee member Nate Higgins ( West Lancashire , 680 votes).A number of Young Greens hold positions in local government including Jamie Osborn , Amy Heley , Hannah Clare , Martin Osborne , Jo Norton , Sara Hall and Nannette Youssef who were elected in the May 2019 elections in the most successful elections thus far for the Young Greens .
