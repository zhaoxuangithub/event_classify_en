[ 57 ] In September 1997 he was a signatory to the foundation of the International Asatru/Odinist Alliance ( IAOA ) , a global union of folkish Heathen groups , alongside Murray of the AA and Heimgest of the British Odinic Rite ; they were later joined by representatives of the French and German branches of the Odinic Rite , before the union terminated several years later .
