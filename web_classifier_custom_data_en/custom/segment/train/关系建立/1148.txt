This is a list of Canadian ambassadors and high commissioners to Ireland . From 1928 to 1949 Canada sent High Commissioners to the Irish Free State , which was a fellow member of the Commonwealth at the time . With the passage of the Republic of Ireland Act 1948 , Ireland left the Commonwealth , and Canada and Ireland have exchanged ambassadors since . The Canadian Embassy is located at 7 - 8 Wilton Terrace in Dublin near St Stephen 's Green . The ambassador lives at 22 Oakley Road , Ranelagh a rowhouse in The Triangle area .
