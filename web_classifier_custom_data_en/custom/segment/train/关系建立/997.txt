Brunswick joined the Zollverein Customs Union in 1842 , while Hanover and Oldenburg finally joined in 1854[25 ] After the Austro-Prussian war of 1866 , Schleswig , Holstein and Lauenburg were annexed by Prussia and thus annexed also to the Customs Union , while the two Mecklenburg states and the city states of Hamburg and Bremen joined late because they were reliant on international trade .
