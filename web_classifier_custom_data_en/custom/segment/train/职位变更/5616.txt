In May 1988 , rehabilitation of Zinoviev , Kamenev , Radek , and co-defendants was announced . After the death of Stalin , Nikita Khrushchev repudiated the trials in a speech to the Twentieth Congress of the Russian Communist Party : The commission has become acquainted with a large quantity of materials in the NKVD archives and with other documents and has established many facts pertaining to the fabrication of cases against Communists , to glaring abuses of Socialist legality which resulted in the death of innocent people .
