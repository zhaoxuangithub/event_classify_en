Singapore hosted the 1st Ministerial Review of the Agreement in November 2001 , while New Zealand hosted the 2nd Ministerial Review of the Agreement in July 2004.The CEP is comprehensive , covering goods , services , investment and technical and hygiene/quarantine barriers to trade in goods . Over the 2 years since the agreement came into effect , Singapore 's bilateral trade with New Zealand rose from $ 0.97 billion ( 2000 ) to S$1.09 billion ( 2002 ) , an increase of 12.6%.Beyond providing improved market access , the ANZSCEP also brings economic cooperation between Singapore and New Zealand to new heights .
