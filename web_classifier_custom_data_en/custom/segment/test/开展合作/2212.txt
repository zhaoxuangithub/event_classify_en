For the DV-Lottery , those who were born in Mainland China are not eligible to apply , whereas persons born in Macau are qualified . The U.S. government supports Macau 's autonomy by strengthening bilateral ties through the promotion of bilateral trade and investment , law enforcement cooperation , academic and cultural links , and high-level dialogue and visits . After the September 11 attacks , Macau officials pledged full cooperation with U.S. and global efforts against terrorism .
