Maximilian formally accepted the crown on April 10, 1864, and set sail for Mexico, arriving in Veracruz on May 28 and reaching the capital on June 12.On his arrival in the summer of 1864 Maximilian declared a political amnesty for all liberals who wished to join the Empire, and his conciliation efforts eventually won over moderate liberals such as José Fernando Ramírez and Santiago Vidaurri [7].
