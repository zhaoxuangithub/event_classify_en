The Roman Catholic Parish of Nuestra Señora de los Remedios ( " Our Lady of Remedies " ) was consecrated in 1924 , and in 1933 , Villa Galíndez and the Argentine Eden were incorporated as Remedios de Escalada ( in honor of María de los Remedios de Escalada , wife of the leader of the Argentine War of Independence , General José de San Martín).Remedios de Escalada was formally declared a city by the Buenos Aires Province Legislature in 1974 .
