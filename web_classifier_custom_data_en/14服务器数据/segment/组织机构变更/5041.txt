In a chronological and theoretical sense , there are classical — those created throughout the 19th century — and post - classical anarchist schools – those created since the mid-20th century and after . Mutualism is an anarchist school of thought which can be traced to the writings of Pierre - Joseph Proudhon , who envisioned a society where each person might possess a means of production , either individually or collectively , with trade representing equivalent amounts of labor in the free market .
