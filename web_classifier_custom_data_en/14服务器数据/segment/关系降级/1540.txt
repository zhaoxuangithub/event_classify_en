The Principality of Bulgaria serviced the debt from 1883 until 1885 , when relations between Bulgaria and Russia were severed after Bulgarian unification with Eastern Rumelia , which had not been approved by Russia , and the following Serbo - Bulgarian War , prior to which Russia had withdrawn its military officers , who had until that moment commanded all larger units of Bulgaria 's young army . In 1890 , it had sent a service payment , but regular payments were resumed only in 1896 .
