Kahane 's legislative proposals focused on revoking the Israeli citizenship for non - Jews and banning Jewish - Gentile marriages and sexual relations , based on the Code of Jewish Law compiled by Maimonides in the Mishneh Torah . As his political career progressed , Kahane became increasingly isolated in the Knesset .
