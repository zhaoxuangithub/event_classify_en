The Protocol of Corfu ( Greek : Πρωτόκολλο της Κέρκυρας , Albanian : Protokolli i Korfuzit ) , signed on May 17 , 1914 , was an agreement between the representatives of the Albanian Government and the Provisional Government of Northern Epirus , which officially recognized the area of Northern Epirus as an autonomous self - governing region under the sovereignty of the prince of the newly established Principality of Albania .
