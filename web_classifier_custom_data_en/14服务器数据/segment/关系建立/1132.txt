The diplomatic mission in London is also accredited to the International Maritime Organization , and the Commonwealth of Nations . Kenya and the UK enjoy cordial relations , and the mission 's mandate is to forge closer relations between the people of Kenya and the people of United Kingdom in pursuit of deeper bilateral and multilateral cooperation in trade and investments , culture , science and technology as well as other fields for mutual benefit . The High Commission is housed in one of a group of Grade II * listed buildings in Portland Place .
